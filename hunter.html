Hunter

Here is the story of her name:

When we got her we had no names picked out since it was a spontaneous decision. But after seeing her play with a toy I knew that she was a hunting dog so thats where the name came from. Also Hunter Mountain is a ski mountain I visited a lot as a child, it is one of my favorite places in the world, so the name is also inspired from that.

Age:

About 7 years old

Here is the story of how Hunter became a part of my family:

On Nikodem's 10th birthday we went into Petco to get a fish tank for our fish. Upon arival we found there was an adoption event going on outside the store, so, as expected, we stopped to look at the dogs. When we saw Hunter my mom, brother and I fell in love. We held her for hours and knew we wanted her. We called my mom and older brother to come see her and as a family we decided to adopt her.

Personality traits:

Energetic and always willing to play. Loves attention and love. Very intitive, she knows when to play and when to be calm.

What does Hunter do all day?

She is the most active of all the pets. She goes on a walk in the woods every morning. She spends a lot of time playing and laying in the backyard.

Who is Hunters favorite family member?

Mom!
