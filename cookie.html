Here is the story of how Cookie became a part of my family:

I loved my first cat matilda so much that I wanted another cat. So... on my tenth birthday I got Cookie! Although she was for the whole family I had the opportunity to name her. After much much thought I came upon the name... Cookie Dough. I loved cookies, so I thought what better to name my cat.

She likes to find where people are sitting and come sit on them. She also enjoys sneaking outside whenever the deck door is open but then running back inside. She likes to eat matildas food even when she has her own. She also likes to fight with hunter.
